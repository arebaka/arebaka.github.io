About

I am Arelive, and I have been moving forward in by life with my keyboard and soldering iron since 6 years old.

I am a software developer and a tech evangelist working on web applications, bots, cloud services, databases, and everything related to server administration.

I got the skills of backend on the stack of Node.js, reactive and legacy frontend, git, Linux systems, desktop GUI, and x86 low-level code. I also got into Arduino, ESP32 recently, and I am learning Android and Go language developing now.
